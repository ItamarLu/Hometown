Visit Juazeiro do Norte

Considered one of the three largest centers of popular religiosity in Brazil, the city also has one of the largest academic centers in the Northeastern countryside.

Three places to visit at Juazeiro do Norte

Basilica of Nossa Senhora das Dores

Built in 1875 by Father Cícero Romão Batista, the Basilica of Nossa Senhora das Dores is one of the most visited places in the municipality of Juazeiro do Norte, in the southern region of the State of Ceará.

Statue of Padre Cícero

The Statue of Padre Cícero on Colina do Horto is a monument built in honor of Padre Cícero. There is a small museum and a church on site. The earlier initial design that the statue would be 27 meters tall.

Federal University of Cariri

The Federal University of Cariri is a Brazilian federal public higher education institution, created in 2013 by the dismemberment of the Juazeiro do Norte campus of the Federal University of Ceará (UFC).
